| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-21-03-01 | DoS | read_file_content (memory) | mitigate | Cap remains in place (potentially raised to 5MB but still bounded). Ensures one click cannot OOM the app. |
