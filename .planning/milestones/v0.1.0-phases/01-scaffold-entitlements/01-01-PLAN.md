not load. Do NOT skip the chunks/ copy step.

Do NOT rename the chunk file — the relative import in arrow.js uses the exact
filename `internal-DchK7S7v.mjs`. Renaming it will break the import.

Do NOT use the CJS bundle (dist/index.js) — it is an IIFE and loses named ESM
exports that components use.
